Local Owned Restaurants Near UMBC

This is a guide to a few local owned restaurants in the UMBC area. Each has their own specialities while all periodically host fundraiser events with student organizations. Pho Saigon offers an excellent pho and a wide variety of Vietnamese cuisines. Taneytown Deli & Sandwich Shoppe has a great selection of both breakfast and lunch sandwiches alongside typical deli options. Sorrento of Arbutus offers great combo specials for their pizza, wings, and a pitcher of beer alongside their wide selection of subs. And finally, Depaola’s Pub has a nice selection of burgers, appetizers, and drinks, while also hosting events like trivia and karaoke.
